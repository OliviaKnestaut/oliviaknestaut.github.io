Olivia is a high school senior who is passionate about creativity, problem-solving, and making technology accessible for all. She attends Cab Calloway School of the Arts High School where she studies Digital media. Since middle school, she has been a member of her school's chapter of Technology Student Association. She has also been a girl scout for over 10 years which has been extremely beneficial in allowing her to experience programs and resources outside of school. She enjoys doing volunteer work in digital media areas, whether that means designing logos for school clubs or helping operate live streams of school events. She is currently applying to colleges where she can study an interdisciplinary program in computer science and media design. As a student with chronic migraines and sensory processing issues, she hopes to encourage the consideration of a range of disabilities throughout the process of media creation. Outside of her technology interests, Olivia loves crocheting and taking care of houseplants.
